How It Works

Our campus navigation system uses your device's camera and GPS to create an augmented reality experience that helps you find your way around campus. Here's how it works:

  1. Select your destination from the list of buildings and rooms
  2. The app will calculate the optimal path from your current location
  3. Follow the augmented reality markers that appear on your screen
  4. View information about buildings, rooms, and other points of interest as you navigate

Room Plaque Recognition

Our newest feature uses computer vision to recognize room plaques in buildings, allowing for precise indoor positioning and navigation. Simply point your camera at a room plaque, and the system will:

  • Identify your current location based on the plaque
  • Calculate an optimal path to your destination
  • Display AR markers to guide you along the path
  • Provide real-time updates as you navigate

Note: For the best experience, use this application outdoors or in large open spaces where GPS signals are strong and you have a clear view of your surroundings.
